## **The Logic Behind Simultaneous Matches**
The primary reason for simultaneous matches is to prevent teams from gaining an unfair advantage by knowing the results of their competitors before playing their own game. This approach ensures that all teams are on an equal footing, with the same amount of pressure and motivation to perform. According to **The Athletic**, this strategy has been in place since the 1990s, when **UEFA** introduced the current format of the **Champions League**. By playing matches simultaneously, **UEFA** aims to maintain the integrity of the competition and promote a level playing field.
